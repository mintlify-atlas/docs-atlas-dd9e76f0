title: Channels

---

The "Channels" dialog allows manipulation of the song's channels.

![channels dialog](/images/8-advanced/channels.png)

Each channel has the following options:

## Channel Options

<ParamField path="Pat" type="checkbox">
  Uncheck the box to hide the channel from the pattern view. Pattern data will be kept.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="Osc" type="checkbox">
  Uncheck the box to hide the channel from the per-channel oscilloscope view.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="Swap/Move" type="button">
  ![crossed arrow button](/images/8-advanced/channels-swap.png)
  
  Click and drag to rearrange pattern data throughout the song. Shift-click and drag to copy pattern data.
  
  <Note>
    This does **not** move channels around! It only moves the channel's pattern data.
  </Note>
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="Name" type="text">
  The name displayed at the top of each channel in the pattern view.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="Short Name" type="text">
  The short name is displayed in the orders view and/or when a channel is collapsed.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="Color" type="color">
  Selects a custom color to be used in pattern view headers and other channel indicators.
  
  ![circle-of-arrows button](/images/8-advanced/channels-reset.png) - Click to reset channel color to default.
</ParamField>
