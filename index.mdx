  <Card title="200+ Sound Chip Presets" icon="microchip">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Pattern-Based Composition" icon="table">

  </Card>

  </Card>
  <Card title="Multiple Export Formats" icon="file-export">

## Quality Emulation

Furnace uses industry-standard emulation cores for accurate sound reproduction:

- **Nuked** - Cycle-accurate Yamaha FM chip emulation
- **MAME** - Multi Arcade Machine Emulator cores
- **SameBoy** - Highly accurate Game Boy emulation
- **Mednafen PCE** - PC Engine / TurboGrafx-16 emulation
- **NSFplay, puNES** - NES and expansion chip emulation
- **reSID, reSIDfp** - Commodore 64 SID emulation
- **Stella** - Atari 2600 TIA emulation
- **ymfm, vgsound_emu** - Modern FM synthesis cores
